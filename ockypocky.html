Ocky Pocky

Project Info:

OckyPocky is a playful KidSAFE Seal Certified interactive learning app designed for preschoolers in the age group of 2-6 years. It comes with easy controls that enable your little one to watch videos without parental assistance, that's more free time for parents.

It not only has fun content for just kids, but also exciting collaborative activities that parents and children can enjoy together.
